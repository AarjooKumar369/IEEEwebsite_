Myron Zucker Design Contest 2016

The Myron Zucker Undergraduate Student Design Contest is open to all IEEE Undergraduate student members. The contest includes two separate categories, individual design contest and team design contest. The selected projects will be awarded by cash prizes, department prizes and travel aids to attend the IAS Annual Meeting.
