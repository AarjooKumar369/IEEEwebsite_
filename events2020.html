  • IEEE IAS-PES Distinguished Lecture on “Condition monitoring and fault diagnosis of electric machines”

    This was the very first event of its kind which was organised to just resume the strength and providing motivation of those affected by the current pandemic. So, IEEE IAS organised an IEEE IAS distinguished Lecture delivered by Prof. Jose A. Antonino-Daviu from Universitat Politecnica de Valencia, Valencia, Spain. He delivered the lecture on the topic of monitoring and fault diagnosis of electric machines. This topic is not only in high demand among industries, but it also plays a critical role in supporting present infrastructure, which is heavily reliant on electrical gear. As a result, concepts of machine monitoring and problem detection needs to be combined to ensure that an industry runs smoothly.
    Speaker:Prof Jose A. Antonino

  • IEEE IES Distinguished Lecture on Smart Microgrid Management

    IEEE PELS-IES Distinguished Lecture was delivered by Mo-Yuen Chow, IEEE Fellow, North Carolina State University, North Carolina, USA on 21st September 2020 on the topic “Smart Microgrid Management”. Smart Microgrids are a well-known framework for using Electrical Energy Storage Systems (EESSs). Energy Efficiency Programs (EEPs), on the other hand, are widely acknowledged as an essential and highly important component of smart MG investments and operations.
    Speaker:Prof. Mo-Yuen Chow (North Carolina State University, North Carolina, USA)

  • IEEE talk on “Cutting Edge Applications of undergraduate control”

    A talk on the topic “Cutting edge application of undergraduate control” was organised by IEEE RAS Student Branch Chapter IIT Mandi on 7th November 2020. The talk was delivered by Ramprasad Potluri, associate professor at IIT Kanpur.The discussion started at 12 p.m. (IST) and ran for 2 hours, with Q&A in between. Following, subtopics such as Path-tracking control of a moon rover using kinematic-based methods, Control Systems Laboratory and its experiments that have already run for 10 years continuously at IITK, built at less than a tenth of the cost of commercially available experimental setups, and page-separation-lifting-turning mechanism of an Automatic book copier were briefly discussed.
    Speaker:Dr. Ramprasad Potluri

  • Collaboration with IIT Mandi Catalyst in organising HST

    HST is IIT Mandi Catalyst's annual flagship event, which aims to bring together stakeholders in the Indian startup ecosystem. HST 2019 attracted 250 attendees, including 52 entrepreneurs that were given the opportunity to connect with investors. Investors, business professionals, and government officials were among the 42 distinguished speakers who discussed a variety of startup-related topics. The 4th edition of HST will bring together a slew of startup professionals and give businesses a reputable venue to propose their ideas for incubation assistance. IIT Mandi catalyst partnered with SB Chapter IIT Mandi for publicity, outreach, and other volunteer assistance. Although this three-day event was entirely virtual, our volunteers assisted as much as they could in conducting the talks, conference and many more.

  • WiE Ideathon 2020

    Women in Engineering Affinity Group of IEEE SB Chapter IIT Mandi organised an Ideathon during 19-20 December, 2020. In this Ideathon, students in a group of 3 had to come up with an innovative tech-based solution that can bridge the gap and streamline the education system so that the learning can be more effective. The event began at 12 p.m. on December 19, 2020 and continued for 48 hours. Participants were required to come up with a solution on the theme "Where education meets technology" and present it using a two-minute video and a presentation. They were compelled to think of some innovative ideas that can really be implemented in this new normal. This event drew around 24 students. The core team members accompanied them throughout the event and offered assistance as required. The participants were ecstatic to be there. They were made to think out of the box. They also learnt how to first analyse all the prospects of an idea before implementing it.
